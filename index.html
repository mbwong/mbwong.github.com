Things I like

I like a lot of things, but especially analyzing large datasets, programming, thinking about product design and distribution, economics, and human computation.

Some of things I've done in the past include Freakonomics author Steve Levitt and also at McKinsey & Co. While school was in session, I worked as a research assistant at the Harvard Physics Department and a teaching fellow for Harvard’s CS51.

I like food from all corners of the world. I enjoy pinot noir, puer tea, and decaffienated coffee. Friends generally classify my musical taste as “indie”, but I enjoy classical, jazz, and occasionally rap as well.

My politics are best described as pragmatic. Broadly speaking, I am a social liberal and a fiscal moderate. If I am unsure about a topic, I generally defer to The Economist newspaper.
