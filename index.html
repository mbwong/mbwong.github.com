About Me

This is what I look like. I know you were curious.

I am interested in using data to understand and improve businesses and public policies. I am employed at APT, where I help large enterprises with experiment design and analysis. My daily work involves measuring the incremental impact of marketing, pricing, operations, or capital initiatives.

I drink tea, not coffee. I bike to work and love Honey-nut Cheerios. I like to make friends smile when they are upset. I grew up in Hong Kong and live in San Francisco.

Apart from my parents, thinkers who influenced me most include Danny Kahneman, Paul Graham, Thich Nhat Hanh, Marcus Aurelius, and Reinhold Niebuhr.
